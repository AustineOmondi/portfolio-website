Services

Architectural Design Services: Conceptual Design: Developing initial design concepts and sketches. Schematic Design: Creating preliminary drawings and layouts. Design Development: Refining the design, incorporating structural and functional considerations.

Space Planning: Determining the optimal layout and organization of interior spaces.

Site Analysis and Planning: Assessing the site's suitability for the intended use. Planning the placement of buildings and infrastructure on the site.

Feasibility Studies: Evaluating the viability of a project, including budget and site constraints.

Programming: Collaborating with clients to define project requirements, goals, and objectives.

Construction Documents: Preparing detailed drawings and specifications for construction. Bidding and Negotiation:

Assisting clients in selecting contractors and managing the bidding process.

Construction Administration: Overseeing the construction process to ensure compliance with the design and specifications.

Interior Design: Planning and designing interior spaces, including finishes, furnishings, and lighting.

Sustainable Design and Green Building: Integrating sustainable and energy-efficient design principles.

Creating 3D models and renderings to help clients visualize the design.

Building Information Modeling (BIM): Commercial and Industrial Design:

Designing offices, retail spaces, factories, and warehouses.

Educational and Institutional Projects: Designing schools, universities, healthcare facilities, and public buildings.

Hospitality and Restaurant Design: Designing hotels, resorts, and dining establishments.

Specialized Design:

Tailoring services to niche areas such as healthcare, transportation, sports facilities, and more. Architectural firms often offer a combination of these services to meet the specific needs of their clients. The scope of services may vary from project to project, and firms can also provide consulting and advisory services for various aspects of the built environment.
